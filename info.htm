format

  • Must include ----[ Recap ]---- exactly as it is
  • Images are transformed to fit 16:9 if they aren't already
  • Exact dimensions: 416 x 234
  • For webm files, I use the thumbnail
  • You do not have to reply to the recap post for the scraper to catch your entry
  • If you don't include an image, it will default to https://my.mixtape.moe/ciuuwa.png

scoring

  • Each week you participate, you gain (5 * multiplier) points
  • Your score multiplier starts at 1 and increases by 0.5 each consecutive week you participate
  • If you miss a week, your multiplier and streak counter will reset
  • Multipliers cap at 10
  • If you want to change your game's title without resetting your score, use the *Game field like so:

----[ Recap ]----
Game: Old Title
*Game: New Title
